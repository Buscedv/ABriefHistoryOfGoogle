#

A brief history of Google

Scroll or click through the history of Google.

1995-2019

1995

1995

Founders Larry Page and Sergey Brin met at Stanford University.

1996

1996

They built a search engine called BackRub, that stood out because of it's use of links to rank websites.

1998

1998

BackRub was renamed to Google (a play on the mathematical term "googol", a 1 followed by 100 zeros).

1999

1999

Google relocated to Mountain View, and they redesigned their logo.

2000

2000

Google introduced AdWords, a self-service tool for creating online ad campaigns.

2003

2003

AdSense launched as a tool for making revenue of ads on your websites.

2004

2004

Gmail was launched on April Fool's Day.

2005

2005

Google acquired the digital mapping company Keyhole, and soon after launched Google Maps & Google Earth.

2006

2006

YouTube was acquired by Google.

2007

2007

The mobile phone operating system "Android" was announced. Streetview launched in the U.S.

2008

2008

The Google Chrome web browser was launched.

2009

2009

Google Announces Chrome OS.

2011

2011

Google introduced the world to Google+.

2015

2015

Google reorganized and the holding company Alphabet Inc. was born.

2016

2016

The AI powered Google Assistant was launched, and Google Announces their new Smartphone: the Google Pixel.

2017

2017

Google restructured to a limited liability company (LLC).

2019

2019

Google shuts down Google+
